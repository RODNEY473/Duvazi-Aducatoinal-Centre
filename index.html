The Beginning of Duvazi Private School

Founded in 2025 under Giyani Municipality, Duvazi Private School has quickly established itself as a center of academic excellence and holistic development.

Our mission is to provide a transformative educational experience that equips students with the knowledge, skills, and values to succeed in an ever-changing world.
